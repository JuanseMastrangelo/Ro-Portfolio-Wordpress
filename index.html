About me

Approach and
philosophy

Rocío Pato
+59894130822
rochiipato@gmail.com
Montevideo, Uruguay

I believe in the invisible—what is felt before it's understood. I draw to name the shapeless, design to build small worlds we can live in. My work emerges from the intersection of instinct and observation, of the intimate and the collective. I don't seek exact answers—I'm drawn to glowing questions, to mistakes that leave a mark, to aesthetics as a form of resistance. My language is visual, but what I try to say resonates beyond the image.
